## 📖 Overview

**Gale** stress-tests the payment core and adds risk controls for high-speed use.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Block high-risk transfer amounts and velocities.
- Preserve risk evidence for administrator review.
- Stress wallet locks, ledger posting, and provider callbacks.
- Optimize transaction-history queries and pagination.

## ✨ Planned scope

### 🛡️ Fraud and performance

- Single, hourly, and daily transfer controls.
- Risk scores, reason codes, and review states.
- Abuse alerts and protected-operation rate limits.
- Stress, load, and query-plan acceptance gates.

## 🏛️ Architecture progress

Risk assessment runs before ledger posting and persists blocked attempts without
partially changing a wallet.
