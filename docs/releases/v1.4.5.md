## 📖 Overview

**Trade** adds request, approval, and scheduled payment workflows.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Request payment from a selected user or QR payer.
- Accept, decline, cancel, expire, and audit requests.
- Schedule one-time and recurring transfers.
- Preserve idempotency and risk checks for automated payments.

## ✨ Planned scope

### 🤝 Payment coordination

- User-targeted and open QR payment requests.
- One-time, daily, weekly, and monthly schedules.
- Background due-transfer processing with failure notifications.
- Business notes, references, status, and cancellation controls.

## 🏛️ Architecture progress

Requests and schedules call the same transfer service as direct payments, so
ledger, risk, notification, and audit rules cannot be bypassed.

## 💻 Compatibility

Node.js 20+ worker runtime · PostgreSQL 14+ · Expo SDK 53
