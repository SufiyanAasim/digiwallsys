## 📖 Overview

**Armada** is the wide-release readiness milestone for the complete ecosystem.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Validate every provider, ledger, fraud, reporting, and admin workflow end to end.
- Complete security, privacy, accessibility, and operational review.
- Prove backup, restore, rollback, and incident-response procedures.
- Establish supported production rollout and monitoring gates.

## ✨ Planned scope

### 🚢 Ecosystem readiness

- Full workflow acceptance and regulated-provider integration review.
- Disaster-recovery drills and recovery-objective evidence.
- Penetration testing, privacy review, and independent financial audit gates.
- Production runbooks, alert ownership, rollback, and support escalation.

## 🏛️ Architecture progress

Armada does not replace ledger or provider controls; it verifies the entire
system operates safely as one monitored, supportable platform.
