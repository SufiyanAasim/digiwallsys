## 📖 Overview

**Passage** secures movement of funds through immutable double-entry accounting.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Record balanced debit and credit entries for every money movement.
- Prevent ledger mutation after posting.
- Link wallet transfers and provider funding to journal references.
- Enforce accounting invariants in PostgreSQL.

## ✨ Planned scope

### 📒 Immutable ledger

- Wallet-liability and provider-clearing accounts.
- Balanced journals and deferred constraint validation.
- Immutable journal/entry triggers.
- UUID references connecting receipts, transactions, and ledger records.

## 🏛️ Architecture progress

Wallet balances become a cached projection; the append-only ledger becomes the
auditable financial record used for reconciliation.

## 💻 Compatibility

PostgreSQL 14+ with `pgcrypto`
