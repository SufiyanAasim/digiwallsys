# Contributor Covenant code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the `digiwallsys` community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for the
community, and show empathy toward others. Harassment, intimidation, doxxing,
and publishing private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may
remove content or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
behavior threatens a safe and productive community.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
